About Us/ჩვენ შესახებ

Welcome to Psychobot!

Psychobot is an innovative platform dedicated to helping individuals improve their mental well-being. Our goal is to provide users with valuable tools and resources that help them understand and manage their emotional and psychological health.

Through our services, we aim to provide mental health support and promote well-being. Our chatbot offers various psychological advice, guidance, and coping mechanisms to users, all available at their fingertips.

Mission & Vision/მისია და ხედვა

Our mission is to make mental health support more accessible, affordable, and available to everyone. We believe that psychological wellness is just as important as physical health, and we want to provide everyone with the tools to take care of their mental health.

Our vision is to build a community where individuals feel supported in their journey toward better mental health, while also fostering open dialogue about mental health issues.
